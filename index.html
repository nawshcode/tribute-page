Seedhe Maut

They represent the next stage in the evolution of the capital's hip-hop sound. Having mastered the art of delivering razor sharp, combative and witty rhymes, the duo are following in the trailblazing footsteps of international hip-hop collectives such as Run The Jewels, Clipse, Black Hippy, Mobb Deep, Blackstar and more.Their passion gets reflected through the songs they make, which made me a true admirer of their work. This page is dedicated to hence the best duo of Indian Hip-Hop scene.
